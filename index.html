AInimate is founded by WANG XINRUI, XIE MENG and ZHANG XIN at June 2024. We aim at providing AIGC driven multimedia interactive entertainment services to ACG fans.

Features
  • Multimedia:
    We present digital ACG characters to users that are visible, hearable and conversational.
  • Interactive:
    Our digital ACG characters are powered by LLM and can dialogue with users.
  • Customized:
    Appearance, voice and personality of characters can be customized by user give reference.
Business Models
  • Entertainment Service Provider:
    We provide multimedia interactive entertainment services of ACG characters for individual users.
  • Digital Human Solution Provider:
    We provide digital human services that could be customized by specific needs from users.
  • UGC Sharing Platform:
    We build a platform for users to share their customized digital characters.
Demo
